Chicken Pizza

Description

We all have days where we want to disregard our health and just eat without a care in the world. In those moments what else would be better than a chicken pizza. A long slice of juicy pizza can make all your problems go away. But it also creates problems health problems. This recipe is fairly healthier than eating from outside but eating in moderation is the most important factor. Enjoy!!

Ingredients

  • 3 cooked chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 (2 ounce) bottle hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle blue cheese salad dressing
  • 1 (16 inch) prepared pizza crust
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  3. Mix chicken, hot sauce, and melted butter in a bowl until well combined.
  4. Place crust onto a rimmed baking sheet or pizza pan. Spread salad dressing over crust.
  5. Top with chicken mixture and sprinkle with mozzarella.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly, about 5 to 10 minutes. Let sit a few minutes before slicing.
